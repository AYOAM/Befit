About Befit

Short Profile

Launched in 2007, Befit is an online and mobile (iOS, Android) calorie and exercise tracker with over 10 million members worldwide. The average active member loses 1.4 pounds a week, and about 27 pounds total.
Befit guides your weight loss journey with accurate calorie counting, weight loss forecasting and personalized daily insights.



Mission

Befit helps people achieve healthy and scientifically proven weight loss for a healthy living.



Numbers & Facts

  • Over 10 million registered users
  • Team of 18 employees (including 5 Registered Dietitians and Diabetes Educators)
  • App Store user rating: 4.7, Google play: 4.6
  • 770,000 verified food labels in the database
